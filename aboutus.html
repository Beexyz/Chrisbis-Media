WHAT CHRISBIS MEDIA EMBODIES

Chrisbis Media is an independent media established in 2020. It prides itself in delivering creative ideas while keeping users and readers up to date on the latest happenings around the world. It started off as an animation, diigital marketing and content creation establishment in 2020 and has now incorporated the news arm in the year 2023.

Chrisbis Media is committed to transparency, accountability, public interest, political neutrality, fact-based reporting and undiluted entertainment amongst others.
